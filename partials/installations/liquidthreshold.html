liquid threshold #1

collaboration project with ali m. demirel and sam friedland

 

This work explores Water's sonic verge/edge. It is the first episode of its series.

The idea of a fringe between two possible dimensions or states of an element came up in FKA residency when considering the concept of Silence as a threshold: its expression can be found in many ways in different contexts, in a composition it works as variation of state/status, as an event of alternation.

Liquid Threshold#1 examines water sounds in two different states/spaces, the border of a possible inception. This work presents the under and over Water and its sonic depth. The space in between finds its expression through Video and the exhibiting room/place.

Video screening and the exhibition room/place works as the mediator that connects both dimensions.

Technical details

Materials 2 speakers with SSD card incorporated looping recorded sound piece synchronised 1 video projector, 1 iron pipe (underwater effect for one of the speakers), 1 ceiling hanger

Dimension variable
