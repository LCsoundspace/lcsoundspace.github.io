xnx

collaboration with music producer xnx

 

Order is repetition of units. Chaos is multiplicity without rhythm.

M.C. Escher

 

The concept behind XNX’s music is to create a ‘chaotic sonic bubble’. I was invited to design a spatial proposal that would best express this sonic statement.

This installation was developed alongside with the artist to be presented at Semibreve the electronic music and digital art festival on its 2014 edition, alongside works such as ‘stroboscope and Linn LM-1’ from Mark Fell.

In the provided room and with a tight budget, the project design displayed a labyrinthic route to immerse the listeners in a sonic maze. To emphasise this layout and its intricacy the illumination given was a synchronized light strobe that reacted to the XNX’s music continuously playing in the background.
