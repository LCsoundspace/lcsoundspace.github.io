2014 | 2015 • index

Index was a weekly Radio-show with live broadcast weekly at ru( radio at 107.9 fm or online.

It focused on electronic music and urged quickly after Metrónomo Radio-show. Me and Irina Grade decided to maintain the same solid team as its antecessor adding a new member to it: Ian Ezerin. The show allowed everyone’s personal choice on the electronic scene: diversity was the goal.

Please follow the link for more info: INDEX

2013 | 2014 • metrónomo

Metrónomo was a weekly broadcast live Radio-show hosted by Irina Grade and me on ru( radio at 107.9 fm or online.

It was mostly focused on electronic dance music. It was ‘on air’ for nearly a year inviting Afonso Macedo and Roberto Mortágua to join us nearly at its end.

Please follow the link for more info: METRÓNOMO

2011 • rádio bambaataa

This Radio-show was broadcast on a weekly basis [ live @ ru( ] and was focused on music production that would relate to or influenced by Kevin Donovan’s work, who entitled himself as Afrika Bambaataa. This project allowed me to open a more wide perspective on this influential scene.

Please follow the link for more info: RÁDIO BAMBAATAA

2008 | 2011 • origami

After few years practising as a radio technician I decided to explore the radio hosting experience: Origami was the first Radio-show I hosted. Radio ru( dedicated two hours of electronic dance music to the Origami listeners every working day. Alongside a great team of people, I was invited to develop my skills as radio broadcaster on a live show and share my choices on this electronic scene.

Please follow the link to explore a few of them: ORIGAMI
