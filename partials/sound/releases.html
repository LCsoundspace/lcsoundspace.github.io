2020 • dis.quiet

Sound recordings composition produced during the sound art educational residency FKA [DE] in the Summer of 2019. A particular day was assigned to each participant to make a 90 seconds of recording. This is my contribution to this serie of recordings that act as an audio catalogue and a log book, which will be pressed into a vinyl entitled Silence with the Consent of Sound.

Released on the 7th, October 2020 supported by Ground e.V.
